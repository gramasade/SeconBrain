# English Speaking Improvement

## Objective

Meningkatkan kemampuan speaking English dari level B1-B2 ke level C1 (advanced) — mampu berbicara secara fluent, confident, dan terstruktur dalam konteks professional maupun sehari-hari.

---

## Assessment Saat Ini

**Self-assessed level:** B1-B2

| Skill | Perkiraan Level | Target |
|-------|-----------------|--------|
| Speaking fluency | B1 | B2+ |
| Vocabulary range | B1 | C1 |
| Pronunciation | B1+ | B2+ |
| Grammar accuracy | B2 | C1 |
| Listening comprehension | B2 | C1 |

*Catatan: assessment awal — perlu diverifikasi dengan tryout atau feedback native speaker.*

---

## Roadmap

### Phase 1: Foundation (B1 ke B2) — estimasi 4-6 minggu
- [ ] **Daily:** 3 new words — catat tipe kata, arti/makna, contoh kalimat berbagai konteks
- [ ] **Daily:** Shadowing 10-15 menit setiap pagi (pilih 1 sumber per minggu)
- [ ] **Weekly Review (Sabtu):** Review vocabulary mingguan + shadowing evaluation
- [ ] Rekam 2-menit monologue tiap hari, review sendiri
- [ ] Deep-dive grammar: tenses, conditionals, passive voice

### Phase 2: Building (B2 ke B2+) — estimasi 6-8 minggu
- [ ] Cari English conversation group (online)
- [ ] Praktik storytelling — deskripsikan kejadian detail
- [ ] Fokus ke connector & transition phrases
- [ ] Weekly 1-on-1 speaking session (iTalki, Preply, atau native speaker)

### Phase 3: Polishing (B2+ ke C1) — estimasi 8-12 minggu
- [ ] Short presentations (5-10 menit) topik technical/kerjaan
- [ ] Debate & argumentation practice
- [ ] Idiomatic expressions & collocations
- [ ] Accent reduction jika diperlukan
